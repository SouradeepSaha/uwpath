Minors and Options
Earth Sciences Minor
Printable Version

Advisors: See Faculty of Science, academic advisors.

An Earth Sciences Minor is available to students in programs within and outside the Faculty of Science.

Successful completion of this minor requires:

  1. 5.0 units that include:
    • 1.5 EARTH units: EARTH 121, EARTH 121L, EARTH 122, and EARTH 122L
    • 2.0 EARTH lecture units, any level
    • 1.5 EARTH lecture units 300- or 400-level
  2. A minimum cumulative EARTH average of 60%.

Notes

  1. ENVS 195 can be used in place of EARTH 122 but EARTH 122L still needs to be completed.
  2. Students enrolled in any Earth Sciences program or in the Environmental Science program with Geoscience or Water Science Specialization are excluded from this Minor.
