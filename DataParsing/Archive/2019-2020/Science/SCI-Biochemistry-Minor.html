Minors and Options
Biochemistry Minor
Printable Version

Advisors: See Faculty of Science, academic advisors.

Successful completion of this minor requires:

  1. 5.0 units distributed as follows:
    • 1.0 BIOL unit: BIOL 130 and BIOL 240
    • 2.0 CHEM units: CHEM 233, CHEM 264, CHEM 265, and CHEM 331
    • 1.0 BIOL elective unit chosen from: BIOL 309, BIOL 331, BIOL 354, BIOL 428, BIOL 431, BIOL 434, BIOL 438, BIOL 439, and BIOL 448
    • 1.0 CHEM elective unit chosen from: CHEM 357, CHEM 430, CHEM 432, and CHEM 433
  2. A minimum cumulative average of 60% in each of the subjects, Biology and Chemistry.

Notes

  1. CHEM 266, CHEM 267, CHEM 237, and CHEM 333 normally will not count towards the Biochemistry Minor.
  2. A Biochemistry Minor will not be awarded together with a Biology Minor and/or a Chemistry Minor.
