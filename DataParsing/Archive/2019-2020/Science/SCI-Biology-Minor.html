Minors and Options
Biology Minor
Printable Version

Advisors: See Faculty of Science, academic advisors. 

Successful completion of this minor requires:

  1. 5.0 units of BIOL with the following condition:
    • 2.5 units must be BIOL at 300-level or higher
  2. A minimum cumulative BIOL average of 60%.

Notes

  1. Students enrolled in the following programs are excluded from this minor: Biochemistry, Biology, Biomedical Sciences, Biotechnology/Chartered Professional Accountancy, Biotechnology/Economics, Environmental Sciences (Ecology or Water Science Specializations), Life Physics (Biophysics Specialization), and Science and Business (Biology, Biochemistry, or Biotechnology Specializations).
  2. A Biology Minor will not be awarded together with a Medical Physiology Minor.
